Who Are We?

This is an initiative by Business Sweden. And who are we? Half-government, half-business. Suits? Yes. But the only thing you really need to know about us is that we help Swedish startups expand their business abroad. Making sure they reach their full international potential. How? Counseling, information, education, tailored services, establishment and providing contacts in 57 different countries.
